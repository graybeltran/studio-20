The Great Divide

The Great Divide is a multimedia project that explores the extremes of education, labor, housing, and identity disparities through the personal narratives of ten New York City residents. In this project you will experience a series of opposing vignettes that showcase the extremes of New York City in the fields of immigration, identity, age, education and housing.
The Great Divide was designed and produced by the graduate students in New York University’s Studio 20 program.
